Interesting Facts about Camels!!

  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • Camels are known as ship of the desert as they are they only means of transport in the desert

Interesting Facts about Chameleons!!

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin coclour is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeons!!

  • Pigeons can detect sounds at far lower frequencies than humans are able to
  • Pigeons were first found in 3000BC in Mesopotamia, modern day Iraq
  • Pigeons have strong muscles used for flying. They can fly at the altitude of 6000 feet

Interesting Facts about Pandas!!

  • With their black and white skin colour, They have great camouflage for their environment.
  • They spend a lot of their day eating, Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  • Giant pandas grow to between 1.2m and 1.5m, and weigh between 75kg and 135kg.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bears!!

  • The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.
  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The world's smallest stitched teddy bear is a mere 0.29 inches tall

Interesting Facts about Flying Cicadas!!

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs. They are about the size of a grain of rice when they drop from a tree branch
  • Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.
  • Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.
